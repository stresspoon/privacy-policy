🧵 Privacy Policy for Threads Post Scraper

Last updated: December 2024

🛡️ Key Point: This extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

1. 📊 Data Collection

We collect ZERO personal information.

Threads Post Scraper does not collect any personal information from users. The extension:

  • ❌ Does not track user behavior
  • ❌ Does not collect browsing history
  • ❌ Does not access personal accounts or login information
  • ❌ Does not store any data on external servers
  • ❌ Does not use cookies or tracking pixels
  • ❌ Does not collect IP addresses or device information

2. 🔄 Data Processing

The extension processes only:

  • ✅ Publicly visible Threads post content - Text content from posts that users can already see
  • ✅ All processing happens locally in your browser
  • ✅ No data leaves your device
  • ✅ No external servers or APIs are contacted

3. 💾 Data Storage

  • 📁 Collected content is only saved as local files on your device
  • 🚫 No cloud storage or remote databases are used
  • 👤 You have complete control over collected data
  • 🗑️ You can delete collected files anytime

4. 🔐 Permissions Explained

Our extension uses minimal permissions:

  • activeTab: Only accesses the current Threads page when you click our icon
  • downloads: Saves collected content as text files to your Downloads folder
  • scripting: Reads visible post content from Threads pages

❗ Important: We do NOT request broad permissions like "all websites" or "browsing history"

5. 🌐 Third-Party Services

This extension:

  • 🚫 Does NOT integrate with any third-party services
  • 🚫 Does NOT send data to analytics services
  • 🚫 Does NOT use external APIs
  • 🚫 Does NOT connect to advertising networks

6. 📱 What We Access

The extension only accesses:

  • ✅ Text content from public Threads posts (only what you can already see)
  • ✅ Page structure to identify post containers
  • ❌ No private messages or personal information
  • ❌ No account credentials or login data
  • ❌ No images, videos, or media files

7. 🎯 Purpose Limitation

This extension is designed solely for:

  • 📚 Personal learning and research
  • 📝 Content organization and analysis
  • 💾 Local backup of publicly visible content

Not for: Commercial use, data resale, or unauthorized distribution

  • 🔍 Always transparent and user-focused

10. 🛡️ Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, traditional data rights don't apply. However:

  • ✅ You control what content to scrape
  • ✅ You control where files are saved
  • ✅ You can uninstall anytime with no trace left behind
